中国二十四节气——立春，冬至 

The 24 Solar Terms of China —— Start of Spring and Winter Solstice

二十四节气是中国传统文化的重要组成部分，生动地反映了季节、气候和物候的变化规律。其中，立春和冬至意义非凡。立春作为首个节气，宣告春天的到来。大地复苏，万物生长，人们满怀希望与活力迎接新的季节。而冬至时，北半球白昼最短、黑夜最长。长期以来，冬至是家庭团聚的节日，人们会享用饺子或汤圆等传统美食，在一年中最寒冷的日子里象征着温暖和团圆。这两个节气文化内涵丰富，不仅指导着农业生产活动，还承载着中华民族世世代代的深厚情感与智慧。

The 24 solar terms, an important part of China's traditional culture, vividly reflect the changing rules of seasons, climate, and phenology. Among them, Start of Spring and Winter Solstice hold special significance. Start of Spring, the first solar term, heralds the beginning of spring. As the earth comes back to life, everything starts to grow, and people welcome the new season with hope and vitality. Winter Solstice, on the other hand, is a time when the days are shortest and the nights are longest in the Northern Hemisphere. It has long been a festival for family reunions, with people enjoying traditional foods like dumplings or tangyuan, symbolizing warmth and togetherness in the coldest days of the year. These two solar terms, full of cultural connotations, not only guide agricultural activities but also carry the rich emotions and wisdom of the Chinese nation across generations.

立春 Start of Spring

立春是二十四节气中的第一个节气，标志着春季的开始。此时，天气逐渐转暖，东风解冻，蛰虫始振，万物开始复苏。在立春时节，人们有迎春、咬春等习俗，迎春是为了迎接春天的到来，表达对新一年的美好期望；咬春则通常会吃春饼、萝卜等食物，寓意着迎接春天的生机与活力。Start of Spring is the first solar term among the 24 solar terms, marking the beginning of spring. At this time, the weather gradually gets warmer. The east wind thaws the ice, hibernating insects start to stir, and all things begin to recover. During the Start of Spring, people have customs such as welcoming spring and biting spring. Welcoming spring is to greet the arrival of spring and express good expectations for the new year. When biting spring, people usually eat spring cakes, radishes and other foods, implying the embrace of the vitality and energy of spring.

冬至 Winter Solstice

冬至是二十四节气中第二十二个节气，拉开寒冬的深邃序幕。这时，白昼最短、黑夜最长，此后阳光渐长。北方冷风凛冽，南方湿寒入骨，万物在严寒中潜藏。在冬至时节，人们有祭祖、吃特色食物等习俗。祭祖是为了缅怀先辈，寄托对先人的追思与敬意；北方吃饺子，避免 “冻耳朵”，南方吃汤圆，寓意团圆美满，这些习俗承载着对温暖、安康的期盼。The Winter Solstice is the 22nd solar term among the twenty-four solar terms, marking the profound beginning of the cold winter. At this time, the daytime is the shortest and the nighttime is the longest, and after that, the duration of sunlight gradually increases. In the north, the cold wind blows fiercely, while in the south, the damp cold pierces to the bone, and all things lie dormant in the severe cold.
